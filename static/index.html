Convos



Convos is a text roulette app that pairs you up with other Brown students based on common interests.


It's all through texting, so you don't even need to install an app! And it will work on any phone! (no smart phone required)

How to play:
Text any of the following commands to (925) 272-0008:

#new - get a new convo (don't worry, this won't end your current convo. It will start a new one on a separate number.)
#end - end your current convo
#help - see your options
When you get matched with a texting partner, just respond to the text to start talking with your texting partner!

More hints...
Convos supports up to three conversations at the same time. You can start a new convo by texting #new to any number. To keep track of your convos, we recommend putting the numbers in your phone as "Convos One", "Convos Two", and "Convos Three". If you've lost track of the numbers to text, here they are:
